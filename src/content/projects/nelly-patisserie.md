A 235 sqm patisserie on a 100m street frontage in Erbil, featuring complete exterior facade and interior space design under Turkish management.

The back kitchen area includes dishwashing, preparation, and cold storage sections. The front of house features a service counter, display cases, and customer seating areas. Open terraces were planned for additional seating capacity.

The project encompassed exterior facade, interior design, and application drawings across all disciplines (architectural, mechanical, electrical), with full turnkey execution.
