A private villa that welcomes visitors with unique symmetrical rope pools and a landscaped area, also featuring an open swimming pool and two open seating areas.

The villa is planned with a basement and 2 floors totaling 2,037 sqm on a 1,360 sqm land area.

**Floor Plan:**
- **Basement:** Turkish bath (hammam), sauna, steam room, beauty salon, maid's rooms
- **Ground Floor:** Large main hall with gallery, reception and dining room, family room, kitchen with open pool view
- **First Floor:** Five en-suite bedrooms with built-in wardrobes and bathrooms

The project covered concept and application drawings, and interior architectural projects across all disciplines (architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical).
