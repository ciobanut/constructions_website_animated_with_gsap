A 250 sqm office space renovation completed in the Gulon Tower building in Erbil. All floors were finished with marble, ceilings feature LED-lit plasterboard, and glass partition walls and kitchen cabinets were installed according to client requirements and project specifications.

The office interior spaces were implemented on a turnkey basis, covering all fit-out and finishing works.
