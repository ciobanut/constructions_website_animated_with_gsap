A luxury mansion situated on 3 combined parcels within a 668-villa complex in Erbil, with basement, ground floor, and first floor totaling 3,360 sqm of construction area.

The central entrance features an arch extending to the roof. The exterior is equipped with precast cornices and decorative elements emphasizing classical grandeur.

**Floor Plan:**
- **Basement:** Indoor pool, jacuzzi, fitness area, hammam, sauna, steam room, massage room, hobby room
- **Ground Floor:** Main entrance hall, guest room, office, living area, kitchen, glass-floored seating area
- **First Floor:** 6 en-suite bedrooms, family living rooms, cinema, kitchen

All disciplines (architectural, mechanical, electrical), interior design and application drawings, and all turnkey execution work were handled by the project team.
