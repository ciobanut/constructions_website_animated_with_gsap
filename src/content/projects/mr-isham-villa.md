A typical villa in Erbil transformed through interior design into a unique residence, meeting all client requirements.

The ground floor features an open-plan reception, family room, and dining area decorated with wall moldings, complemented by a fireplace, wall cabinets, shelves, a TV wall, a hot kitchen, and a guest bedroom.

The first floor contains three en-suite bedrooms and a large master bedroom with its own dressing room, bathroom, and front-facing balcony.

All floors except wet areas use wood parquet. Wet areas feature selected sanitary ware and custom-designed mirrors.

The project encompassed interior design and application drawings across all disciplines (architectural, mechanical, electrical), with full turnkey execution.
