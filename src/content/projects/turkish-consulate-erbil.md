A turnkey project for the Turkish Consulate in Erbil, encompassing interior architectural designs and the planning and implementation of a new waiting and security building.

Bulletproof glass was used on the exterior for security. Railings were installed between the security and office buildings to control access. Two separate embassy offices are connected internally from within. The project includes a security control office, waiting area, and consulate offices. The total construction area is 380 square meters.

All interior design, 3D visualizations, and turnkey implementation were handled across all design and application disciplines.
