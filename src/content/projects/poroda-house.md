We are delighted to present Poroda, a stunning classical apartment located in the heart of Baghdad, Iraq. Designed and built with the utmost care and craftsmanship, this residence represents a landmark achievement in our portfolio — a home that blends the sophistication of classical architecture with the comfort of modern apartment living.

This apartment has been thoughtfully designed to accommodate a young family, with spaces that balance grandeur and intimacy. The spacious living room seamlessly connected to a gourmet kitchen, a dedicated cinema room, and elegantly appointed bathrooms and technical spaces.

Is dedicated to private family quarters, including a luxurious master bedroom with a walk-in wardrobe and en-suite bath, a study for quiet contemplation, children's rooms, a guest suite, and a dressing room. Every space has been carefully planned to maximize comfort and functionality within this exclusive apartment residence.

The interior design draws on classical design principles — balanced proportions, elegant moldings, and refined detailing — while offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Poroda is more than an apartment; it is a sanctuary that our team built with passion and precision for its distinguished owners.
