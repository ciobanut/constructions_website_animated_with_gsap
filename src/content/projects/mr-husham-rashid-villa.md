A private villa located in Erbil's Aram Village complex, with 2 floors, an open pool, and terrace seating, spanning a total construction area of 1,382 sqm on a 1,080 sqm land area.

The exterior is designed with classical and elegant forms, with the entire facade decorated with precast elements and ornaments.

The ground floor features a grand one-way spiral staircase in the main entrance hall welcoming you into the home. The right side contains the reception and dining area and a guest bedroom. The left side offers family living and dining with pool views, kitchen, and terrace seating.

The first floor comprises four en-suite bedrooms, a hammam, sauna, steam room, and a fitness area with open pool views.

Interior detail drawings, rough construction works, and interior application works were all delivered on a turnkey basis across all disciplines (architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical).
