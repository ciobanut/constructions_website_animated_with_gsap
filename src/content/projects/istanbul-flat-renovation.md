A sea-view 197 sqm 3+1 flat in Istanbul's Etiler district underwent a complete interior design, decoration, and renovation delivered turnkey to the client.

Since the existing structure was very old, all electrical and mechanical infrastructure was renewed — including a smart home system, concealed LED and spot lighting, underfloor heating, camera and security system, thermally insulated aluminum joinery, motorized blinds, complete wet area renewal, and all fixed furniture design and on-site assembly.

The entrance hall, kitchen, and living room floors feature black-bordered white large-format marble installed as bookmatch. Belvedere granite was chosen for kitchen countertops and the island. Wet areas used marble-look ceramics and premium-brand sanitary ware.

The project encompassed all disciplines (architectural, mechanical, electrical), interior design and application drawings, and full turnkey execution.
