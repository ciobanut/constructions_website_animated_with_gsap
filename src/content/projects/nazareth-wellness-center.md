A luxury wellness center planned under the Park View Residential Complex, featuring a swimming pool, steam and sauna rooms, fitness hall, multi-purpose sports room, and crossfit room.

The project spans a total construction area of 2,210 square meters, using first-quality modern materials throughout — from the entrance hall to the lockers.

The 20m × 8.75m swimming pool uses 2.5×2.5cm mosaic ceramic tiles. The sauna features Abachi Ayous wood, while the steam room uses acrylic material. Pool columns and beam faces are covered with white acrylic, and sun lounger decks use iroko wood to create a warm atmosphere. Two jacuzzis and a smoking area are planned within the pool area.

The pool ceiling features galaxy-printed PVC stretch fabric with fiber optic lighting. The crossfit and multi-purpose rooms use linear lighting, while hallways and entrance ceilings feature decorative concealed-lit plasterboard designs.

**Floor Plan:**
- **Ground Floor:** Entrance hall, offices, changing rooms, showers, steam rooms, saunas
- **First Floor:** Fitness room, VIP fitness, multi-purpose sports room, crossfit room, lockers, showers, WC

All concept interior design, application drawings, and turnkey execution were handled across all disciplines (architectural, mechanical, electrical).
