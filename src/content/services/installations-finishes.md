In luxury construction, the difference between exceptional and ordinary is measured in millimetres. Our installations and finishes division is staffed by artisans and technicians who understand that a light switch placed one degree off level, a grout line inconsistent by half a millimetre, or a paint finish with the slightest orange peel is not merely a flaw — it is a failure of standards. We do not fail standards.

Our MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) installations are engineered for resilience and intelligence. Electrical systems are configured for full home automation — Lutron and Crestron integration, zoned lighting, motorized shading, and whole-house audio distribution. Plumbing systems feature Hansgrohe and Watermark fixtures, recirculating hot water loops for instant delivery at every tap, and drainage systems designed for silent operation. HVAC is zoned per room with VRV technology, dehumidification, and air purification — essential in Iraq's urban environment.

Finishes are where we excel. Our flooring division installs wide-plank French oak, book-matched marble, and hand-laid terrazzo with microscopic attention to alignment and acoustics. Plastering is executed in multiple coats to achieve surfaces that appear seamless — walls that catch light like silk. Painting is done by hand, with multiple spray-and-brush passes for depth and durability. Joinery — kitchens, wardrobes, panelling — is fabricated in our own workshop and installed to furniture-grade tolerances.

We believe that a building's true quality reveals itself not in the grand gesture but in the thousands of small decisions made well. Every socket aligned, every corner perfectly mitred, every surface that begs to be touched. This is the standard we hold ourselves to — and the standard our clients have come to expect. In Iraq's emerging luxury market, we are proud to set the benchmark for what finishes can and should be.
