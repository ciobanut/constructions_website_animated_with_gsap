In a city rich with architectural heritage, many of Iraq's finest properties deserve more than demolition — they deserve transformation. We specialize in the sensitive renovation of existing structures, preserving their character while elevating them to modern luxury standards. Whether it is a classical Baghdad villa from the golden era or a 1990s Erbil residence awaiting its renaissance, our team approaches each renovation as an act of curation.

Our renovation process begins with a forensic assessment of the existing structure — identifying opportunities to reimagine spaces, upgrade infrastructure, and resolve decades of wear with surgical precision. We handle everything from complete interior gut renovations to targeted interventions: reimagined kitchen suites with Gaggenau appliances, spa-inspired bathrooms clad in Calacatta marble, and intelligent lighting systems that transform the atmosphere of a room at the touch of a button.

Structural reconfigurations are our specialty. We remove load-bearing walls with carefully engineered steel reinforcement, add floors to existing structures, and carve out dramatic double-height spaces where previously there were none. All work is performed while maintaining the highest standards of dust control and site safety — because many of our clients continue to occupy their properties during renovation.

The result is a home that feels entirely new yet retains the soul of its original architecture. We extend the same approach to commercial renovations, transforming hotels, restaurants, and executive offices into spaces that compete with the best in the region — often without the disruption of a full closure. Our track record in rehabilitation of luxury properties across Iraq is unmatched, with each project receiving the same meticulous attention as a new build.
