Architecture in Iraq carries a profound legacy — from the ziggurats of Ur to the modern masterpieces of Baghdad's mid-century golden age. Our design practice honours this heritage while embracing a distinctly contemporary vision. We create buildings that feel timeless yet unmistakably current, drawing from both the region's rich architectural vocabulary and the global language of modern luxury.

Our design process is intimate and collaborative. We begin not with blueprints but with conversations — understanding how our clients live, work, and entertain. From these discussions emerge concept designs that marry the client's aspirations with our technical expertise. Every project is developed through multiple iterations, presented via photorealistic 3D visualizations and virtual walkthroughs that allow clients to experience their space before a single stone is laid.

We offer a full suite of architectural services: conceptual design, schematic development, construction documentation, structural engineering, MEP engineering, and interior architecture. Our team holds licenses and certifications recognized by Iraqi and international authorities, ensuring that every design complies with local building codes while achieving global standards of excellence. We manage the entire permitting process on behalf of our clients, liaising with municipal authorities so that construction can proceed without administrative delays.

Sustainability is woven into our design philosophy. We specify passive cooling strategies suited to Iraq's climate, specify low-carbon materials wherever possible, and integrate smart building systems that optimize energy consumption without sacrificing comfort. The result is architecture that is not only beautiful and functional but responsible — designed for a future that Iraq is building today.
